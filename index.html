Diamond Lake Pram Fleet

Learning to Sail is Easier Than You Think!!!

Join us and learn the life long skill of sailing. We have the instructors, the boats, and the water. What we need is you!

The Diamond Lake Pram Fleet is made possible by a partnership with Lake Wales Pram Fleet.

Not only does our club have over 200 graduates of our program and regatta wins across Minnesota, but our instructors are some of the most decorated in the country!!!

Sign Up here on the website or meet us at the Diamond Lake Boathouse Saturdays at 11 AM, May to September
